### 雅思阅读八大考点

- 转折
- 因果

- 例子
- 特殊符号

### Scanning阅读技巧

总体思想：找关键词

- 方法：

  1. 关键词（题目的）

  2. 回到原文进行同义替换

     1. 解释（功能/情感色彩）
     2. 近义词

     同义替换可能会有多个情况

- 关键词可能是：

  1. 数字/百分比
  2. 主语（限定：环境/情感/特质）
  3. 国别（大写：项目/人名/特指）
  4. 比较（区分）
  5. 态度/想法



- 简答题做题方法：
  1. 确定字限
  2. 预判答案类型
  3. 圈画题干关键词（2题1组）
  4. 扫读原文定位（标题段落定位）
  5. 细读答案句找同义替换，确定照抄答案
  6. 检查拼写/词义/语义
- 注意：
  - 字限：数字 ——→ `1,2,3/one,two,three`都可以
  - 缩略词形式不考（they're）
  - 连字符的单词算一个单词（eazy-going）
  - 不能缩写



频率副词：

|  100%  |   90%   |       80%        |       70%        |   50%    |     30%      |  10%   |         5%         |  0%   |
| :----: | :-----: | :--------------: | :--------------: | :------: | :----------: | :----: | :----------------: | :---: |
| always | usually | normal/generally | often/frequently | sometime | occasionally | seldom | rarely/hardly ever | never |



### Skimming阅读技巧

题目出现：gist / main idear / title / subtitle

形容词+主语

标题/引入

预判文章内容

需要关注：

- 开头段 ——→ 中心问题
- 每段的第1~2句（实词部分），每段中心问题，结尾段



- 同义替换：
  1. 解释
  2. 近义词/反义词
  3. 词性+主被动
  4. 上义词（包括）/下义词（种类/例子）
- 注意：
  - 主宾倒置（混乱）
  - 逻辑问题（因果）
  - 同样动作不同含义
  - 普遍/特殊

### 几选几

步骤：

1. 确定单选或多选
2. 圈画题干关键词
3. 扫读原文定位
4. 细读答案句找同义替换    ——→注意：同义替换关键词常以近义词出现
5. 再读题干选择匹配选项

### 选人名

做题步骤

1. 读题目要求 (NB)
2. 圈划所有题千关键词 （预测替换）
3. 从A段读起，利用关键词定位答案句
4. 细读答案句匹配同义替换，选择正确选项

### 地图题

主体思想：

1. 相对位置（参照物）
2. 绝对位置（整个环境）

- 名词 + be located / situated / sited + 位置 （situated：主语为大型综合设施/名胜）
- There be + 名词 + 位置
- 相对位置：…，with + 名词 + 位置（相对位置）
- 改建词描述变化为一般过去时的被动语态：名词 + was / were + 改建词的过去分词
- 扩建说法：
  - is / are predicted to
  - is / are projected to
  - is / are expected to
  - is / are estimated to
  - is / are forecast to



写作顺序：

- introduction（改写题目）：

  模板：

  - The two maps show changes to + 地图的描述对象 + 时间
  - The maps compare the layout of + 地图的描述对象 + between … and … / from … to … 

- overview（总结图表最显著的特点）：

  模板：

  - Overall , it is clear that … / we can see that …
  - 地图的描述对象是否发生了许多变化？
  - 新增/拆除明显区域（40%以上）可能不止一个
  - 如果有：交通便利性，连接性

- 按照时间分段：变迁前 + 后（位置）| 按照功能分段：区域1（位置 + 变化）

- 按照时间分段：变迁前 + 后（变化）| 按照功能分段：区域2（位置 + 变化）



常用介词：

- in front of | to the right | in the top left-hand corner | the far left of the campus | opposite
- was relocated | increased in size | were moved | was built | were demolished | created | developed | changed | expanded

记忆：六种变化类型：

1. 移：relocated / moved
2. 扩：developed / increased in size （function 功能）
3. 新：created / built
4. 除：demolished
5. 缩：shrink（shrunk）
6. 转：be convertrd into

注意：

- and（并列）：两个设施发生相似变化 + 一个设施又发生了变化
- but（转折）：一个设施有不同变化 / 没有变化，而另一个设施新 / 除

### 动态图

- 动态图（时间点两个以上）：描述趋势（重点描述：90%以上） + 对比数据

  动态图趋势：

  1. 总体趋势
  2. 上升或下降次数
  3. 上升或下降幅度

- 静态图（0个或1个时间点）：对比数据

  - 最大值/第二大值
  - 合并近似值
  - 最小值
  - 大小比较/倍数比较
  - 集合类信息（其他）

  需要注意：题目可能有多个变量，那么这个的overview需要：最大值 + 最小值（差异性质，根据变量进行分类）

注意：

- 如果一个图中既有静态图又有动态图，那么只需要描述动态图部分。
- 在图中如果有1~2个点有反转（就是A线完全比B线高，但其中1~2个点B比A高）



动态图写作重点：

- 描述趋势：

  上升 / 下降 / 不变(remin / stable) / 波动 / 稳定

  描述趋势时需要：

  - 数据佐证：起点，终点，极值点，交点，拐点，近似点，水平值

    1. 描述交点：
       1. intersect + at + 数据（两条线对象词都要写）
       2. Overtake （一条线超过另一条线 / 取代 / 居上）
    2. 描述拐点：start to decline（一个时间点之前一直有上升趋势，在这个时间点之后一直有下降趋势）

  - 总体趋势：基本不变？（更具起点和终点判断）

  - 局部趋势：

    % of non-graduates：先下降后上升
    
    % of recent graduates：先下降再不变后上升

- 对比数据：

  最大值，最小值，近似值（分别描述这几条线的基数 / 分别趋近与哪一个值）
  
  % of non-graduates **一直高于**% of recent graduates



| 趋势程度 | 形容词 adj.                                                  |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 弱/慢的  | slow，slight，minor                                          |
| 中等的   | moderate 中等的，modest 适度的，steady 稳定的，gradual 逐渐的 |
| 快速的   | dramatic，rapid，sharp，steep                                |
| 大幅度的 | significant，marked，substantial，considerable               |
| 不变的   | stable，unchanged                                            |

介词总结：

- 时间介词：

  - from 2000 to 2020
  - between 2000 and 2020
  - in 2020/October
  - during/in the 1990s
  - during/over/throughout the period

- 数据介词：

  - of：30% of students，an increase of 10%
  - at：peak at，bottom out at，remain stable at
  - in：a significant increase in + petrol prices（对象词）
  - to 到
  - by 了
  - from … to …
  - between … and …

  

重点句式：

1. 对象词（主语 + 计量单位）+ v（变化）+ adv + 介词 + 数据 + 时间

   实例：

   - The number of new entrants to (主语) (趋势) during the period
   - The percentage of new entrants to (主语) (趋势) to xx%

2. There was / has been + a + adj + n（变化）+ in + 对象词 + 介词 + 数据 + 时间

   There was a () in numbers from ()  

3. 对象词 + showed/recorded + a(n) + adj + trend + 介词 + 时间

4. 对象词 + remined / kept unchanged / stable + of + 数据 + 时间

5. 积累：

   - There be + 数据 + in A.
   - There be + three times / twice + more + 对象 + in A + than + in B.（某某数量是某某的几倍）

1. introduction（改写）：

   - ctart / araph → 对应的国表名称 bar chat / pie chart / line graph / table
   - show → illustrate, demonstrate, provide information about, compare（注意三单）
   - 对系词 → 同义替换**或**宾语从句(the number / amount of xxx → how many / much…)
   - between 1980 and 2000 → from 1980 to 2000 / during a 20-year period

2. Overview（总结）：

   1. 日类合并整体趋势（起点和终点）

   2. 整个时间段 / 绝大多数时间段都是最大值的对象

   3. 举例：

```
Examiner: Can you tell me about your hometown?

Candidate:

Absolutely, I'd love to. My hometown is Xinjiang, a vast and culturally diverse region in the northwest of China. It's a place that's rich in history, natural beauty, and cultural heritage.

Xinjiang is known for its stunning landscapes, which include vast deserts, towering mountain ranges, and lush valleys. The region is home to the Taklamakan Desert, one of the largest sandy deserts in the world, and the Tian Shan mountains, which offer breathtaking views and excellent hiking opportunities. The diversity of the landscape is truly remarkable, and it makes Xinjiang a unique and fascinating place to live and visit.

One of the most distinctive aspects of Xinjiang is its cultural diversity. The region is home to many different ethnic groups, including the Uyghurs, Kazakhs, Hui, and Han Chinese. This cultural mosaic is reflected in the local cuisine, music, and festivals. For instance, Uyghur cuisine, which includes dishes like lamb kebabs, pilaf, and naan bread, is incredibly flavorful and has become popular throughout China. Music and dance are also integral parts of the local culture, with traditional Uyghur dancing being particularly vibrant and expressive.

Another noteworthy feature of Xinjiang is its historical significance. The region was a crucial part of the ancient Silk Road, serving as a bridge between the East and the West. This rich history is still evident today in the many historical sites scattered across the region, such as the ancient city of Jiaohe and the ruins of Gaochang. These sites offer a glimpse into the past and are a testament to the region's historical importance.

Living in Xinjiang also means experiencing a unique climate. The region has a continental climate, characterized by hot summers and cold winters. This can be quite challenging at times, but it also means that we get to enjoy a variety of seasonal activities. In the summer, we often go hiking in the mountains or visit the many lakes and rivers for a refreshing swim. In the winter, skiing and snowboarding are popular activities in the mountainous areas.

In terms of development, Xinjiang has made significant progress in recent years. The infrastructure has improved considerably, with new roads, railways, and airports making it easier to travel within the region and to other parts of China. Additionally, the local economy has been growing steadily, with industries such as agriculture, mining, and tourism playing important roles.

Overall, Xinjiang is a place that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and historical significance. It's a region that continues to evolve and develop, while still preserving its distinct heritage and traditions. I'm incredibly proud to call Xinjiang my hometown, and I always look forward to sharing its many wonders with others.
```
